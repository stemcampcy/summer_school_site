About the programme

STEM Summer School Cyprus is a 5-day programme for ambitious high-school students who want to explore advanced STEM subjects in an accessible, practical and inspiring format. The school combines lectures, guided workshops, hands-on activities and small-group projects.

STEM Summer School Cyprus offers

  • Insight on cutting edge STEM topics
  • Hands-on labs, demos, coding sessions and project work
  • Teaching across multiple scientific and technical tracks
  • Mentoring from researchers, engineers and university students
  • A selective cohort of motivated peers
  • A concluding hackathon and showcase of student work

Topics and tracks

Quantum Science & Technology

Introduction to quantum ideas, experiments and emerging technologies.

Embedded Systems

Computer architecture, programming languages, embedded C++ and practical hardware-software integration.

Python & AI

Programming foundations, data analysis, intelligent systems, and applied computational problem solving.

Bioengineering

Biological systems, engineering approaches and innovation in the life sciences.

Mathematical Thinking & Research Skills

Structured reasoning, modelling, experimental design, teamwork and scientific communication across STEM disciplines.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Connecting scientific ideas to real-world applications, product thinking and early-stage business perspectives.

Communication & Presentation

Explaining technical ideas clearly, collaborating effectively and presenting project outcomes with confidence.

How students will learn

Learn the ideas

Short lectures and guided sessions introduce the core concepts behind each track.

Build and test

Students work through labs, coding exercises, electronics activities and experimental demonstrations.

Create and present

Frequently asked questions

Who can apply?

High-school students aged 15–19 with a strong interest in STEM are encouraged to apply.

Do I need prior experience?

While the syllabus is designed to support motivated learners of different backgrounds, prior experience in coding (e.g., Python, C) will be valuable.

How selective is admission?

Places are limited and selection is based on motivation, fit and available capacity.

What is the cost of attendance?

The cost of is €300, which covers attendance, lunch, and snacks throughout the day.

Are there any scholarships available?

Some places for exceptional candidates may be subsidised.

When is the application deadline?

Applications are accepted until 31/05/2026.

Do you offer a transportation service to/from the School?

Depending on enrollment, we may be able to coordinate carpool groups or provide shuttle service to nearby areas. Let us know your transportation needs on the application form, and we'll do our best to accommodate them.
